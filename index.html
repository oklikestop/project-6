Gel Electrophoresis

Gel electrophoresis is a technique used to separate DNA fragments according to their size. source
  1. isolate DNA and preprocess it, then mix in dye to help visualize the movement of the sample through the gel.
  2. TAE buffer provides a source of ions for setting up the *electric field* during electrophoresis.
  3. The weight-to-volume concentration of agarose in TAE buffer is used to prepare the solution.
